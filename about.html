Professional Summary

I am currently working as an SDET-2 at SaaS Labs, where I focus on automating end-to-end workflows, improving testing efficiency, and ensuring high-quality product releases. I use Playwright and TypeScript to automate UI and API end-to-end tests, and I actively mentor junior team members while contributing to sprint planning.

Before this, I worked at Paytm in the Finance & Payments domain, where I improved regression suite execution time by 3 hours and developed a new UI testing framework using Karate. My previous role as an SDET at Mindfire Solutions in the Healthcare domain involved designing and maintaining automation frameworks with Rest Assured and performing performance testing with Python, MongoDB, and ElasticSearch.

Earlier, I contributed to Accenture's Finance & Banking projects, where I enhanced test coverage, performed API testing, and handled data migration.
